Cantonese Lesson
Section 2
Lesson 2.2 - Months (January - December)
Lesson Materials
January yāt yuht
Febuary yih yuht
March sāam yuht
April sei yuht
May ng yuht
June lok yuht
July chāt yuht
August baat yuht
September gáu yuht
October sahp yuht
November saph yāt yuht
December saph yih yuht
Hints:
yuht means month.
yāt means 1. Therefore,
yāt yuht means the first month of the year,
yih means 2. Therefore,
yih yuht means the second month of the year.
etc.
