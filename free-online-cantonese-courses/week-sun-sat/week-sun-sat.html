Cantonese Lesson
Section 2
Lesson 2.1 - Weeks (Sunday - Saturday)
Lesson Materials
Sunday sing keih yaht
Monday sing keih yāt
Tuesday sing keih yih
Wednesday sing keih sāam
Thursday sing keih sei
Friday sing keih ng
Saturday sing keih lok
Hints:
sing keih means day.
yat means 1. Therefore,
sing keih yāt means the first day of the week,
yih means 2. Therefore,
sing keih yih means the second day of the week.
Interesting. Ah?
Caution:
Sunday do not pronounce as sing keih chat. Sunday is pronounce as sing keih yaht (yaht means Sun), i.e. day of the Sun!
