Lesson 4.1 - Color 1
Lesson Materials
Red hǖng sík
Orange chaang sík
Yellow wòng sík
Green luk sík
Blue làahm sík
Purple jí sík
Black hāk sík
White baahk sík
Hints:
sík means color.
hǖng means Red. Therefore,
hǖng sík means Red color.
etc.
