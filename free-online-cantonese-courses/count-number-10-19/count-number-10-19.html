Lesson 1.2 - Numerals (Counting from 10 to 19)
Lesson Materials
10 sahp   15 sahp-ng
11 sahp-yāt   16 sahp-lok
12 sahp-yih   17 sahp-chat
13 sahp-sàam   18 sahp-baat
14 sahp-sei   19 sahp-gáu
Hints:
As you should noticed that the number system in Cantonese is very easy to learn. Once you learn how to pronounce from one to ten, the other numbers are simply pronounce one by one. For example: 3 is sàam, 10 is sahp, 13 is then sahp sàam.
