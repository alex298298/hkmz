About Us

Hong Kong Media Zone (HKMZ) is a small language center in Hong Kong which specialises in Cantonese and Putonghua Learning programmes for locals and overseas learners. Private Learning Programs are welcomed to suit your needs.

Our Mission:
- to foster a perfect learning atmosphere for language learning
- to make language learning easy and fun
- to help learners of all ages to speak Cantonese and Putonghua easily
- to promote and enable all learners to gain insight into Chinese cultures
- to develop their communicative competence in Cantonese and Putonghua
