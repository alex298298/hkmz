Learning Cantonese is Fun and Easy

Cantonese is one of the five major languages in China and is widly spoken by more than 60 million people Cantonese in the world - Southern China, Hong Kong, Macau, Malaysia, Singapore, Canada, United States, Australia, New Zealand and other countries.

You may noticed that Voice of America (VOA) has programs of Chinese languages: one is Cantonese while the other is Putonghua.

Southern China and Hong Kong have a lot of great foods. After you learn Cantonese, you can chat with interesting people and enjoy great dishes.
Why Cantonese is So Popular

Cantonese is the mainly spoken language in Chinese communities in every corner of the world because many Chinese immigrants came from Southern China and Hong Kong.
